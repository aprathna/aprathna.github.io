Soundify

Taking the best of both worlds.

Soundify was created as a team project for a design class at UCSD. In learning about user experience and what really works best, we decided to take what we already knew was good from popular pre-exitsing music steaming mobile apps, Spotify and Soundcloud, and design an app that could put all of those together. Hence, Soundify.

Mint

Our Mission: To provide our users with information they can utilize while making decisions for their future in terms of applying for college.

Created along with a team for a design lab at UCSD, Mint is an app created to answer the question, "Is school worth it?" We bring dynamic visualizations of different data pertaining to education and income in the San Diego area. This helps users visualize and conclude whatever possible correlations that may exist when deciding on whether or not to pursue college.
